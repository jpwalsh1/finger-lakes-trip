# Trip Memory — Walsh Family Finger Lakes Weekend

Reference notes for the trip. Keep in sync with `index.html` if anything
changes (dates, lodging, restaurant picks, etc.).

## Overview

- **Who:** Walsh family
- **When:** Thursday–Sunday, October 15–18, 2026 (long weekend)
- **From:** 7101 Rob Roy Dr, Dublin, OH 43017
- **To:** 1108 East Lake Rd, Dundee, NY 14837 — Keuka Lake lakefront
  cottage (Airbnb, self check-in via smartlock, check-in after 4:00 PM,
  checkout before 10:00 AM)
- **Distance:** ~555 miles one way, ~7.5 hrs driving
- **Thursday, Oct 15, 2026 (arrival day) is the Walsh's 10th wedding
  anniversary** — the itinerary calls this out; Thursday dinner pick was
  upgraded to The Switzerland Inn (on-the-water, sunset views) for the
  occasion.

## Planning history / decisions made

- Original idea included Cooperstown (Baseball Hall of Fame) and a
  possible Seneca/Cayuga Lake cabin, but the Cooperstown ↔ Finger Lakes
  drive turned out to be a ~3 hour backtrack (not the ~40 min originally
  estimated), and Dublin↔Cooperstown is ~555 mi / 8.5 hrs (not 6.5 hrs as
  first estimated). Since the Hall of Fame was mainly of interest to one
  family member and the family was lukewarm on the extra driving,
  **Cooperstown was cut** in favor of a pure, unhurried Finger Lakes trip.
  Cooperstown may be worth its own separate trip in the future.
- National Soccer Hall of Fame is **not** in Ithaca — it relocated to
  Frisco, TX (Toyota Stadium) after the Oneonta, NY location closed in
  2010.
- National Women's Hall of Fame — verify current location if this ever
  gets added back into an itinerary; it was considered but not included
  in the final trip.
- Settled on a lakefront cottage on **Keuka Lake** (not Seneca or
  Cayuga), address above.
- Trip shifted from a Fri–Mon plan to **Thu–Sun** to get more full lake
  days without a Cooperstown detour eating into the schedule.
- Sunday drive home: no restaurant lunch stop — food is grabbed at the gas
  stop only, to keep the return trip simple.

## Gas stops

- Car's range tops out around 300–350 miles per tank.
- One stop each way is sufficient for the 555-mile drive.
- Landing point: **Erie, PA** area, roughly the 300–350 mile mark from
  Dublin (and mirrored on the way back). Use a travel center (Sheetz,
  Pilot, etc.) right off I-90 for fuel + quick food — no sit-down stop.

## Day-by-day summary

**Thursday — Drive to Keuka Lake**
- Depart Dublin 7:00 AM
- Gas/food stop near Erie, PA (~11:30 AM)
- Arrive cottage ~4:00–5:00 PM, self check-in
- Dinner out in Hammondsport

**Friday — Keuka Lake & Hammondsport**
- Glenn H. Curtiss Museum and/or Finger Lakes Boating Museum
- Wineries: Bully Hill, Keuka Lake Vineyards, Dr. Konstantin Frank
- Lake / hot tub time in the afternoon
- Dinner out in Hammondsport

**Saturday — Watkins Glen day trip**
- Watkins Glen Gorge Trail (Rainbow Falls, Cavern Cascade) — go early
- Lunch in Watkins Glen
- Optional: Taughannock Falls if energy allows
- Lake / hot tub time back at the cottage
- Dinner in

**Sunday — Drive home**
- Pack the night before — checkout is a firm 10:00 AM
- Depart 10:00 AM
- Gas/food stop near Erie, PA (~1:30–2:00 PM), no separate lunch stop
- Arrive home ~5:30–6:00 PM
